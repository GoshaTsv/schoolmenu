Throughout many hours of work our experts made the best school menu that is possible. We have 4 sections: Hot meals, Salads, Drinks and Desserts.

In the hot meals section, students can choose what will their main dish be, as we combined taste and health to create this variation of food we hope they will like, it includes: - Aspic (Meat jelly)

- Borsch (Ukrainian soup)

- Mashed potatoes with meatballs

- Mozzarella pizza

- Burgers with ham or chicken.

Salads can be very tasty if made properly! We have 3 delicious salads that students may get for their meal. They are:

- The olivier salad

- The caesar salad

- The avocado salad

Drinks are necessary for a meal to be complete! We have 3 drinks in the menu:

- Milk

- Juice (Multifruit, Apple, Orange, Pineapple)

- Black or Green Tea

And lastly, like in any meal, we have desserts! We also have 3 sweet desserts:

- Tiramisu cake

- Napoleon cake

- Lemoncake
